AMALIJA BLURRY | #ersatzGirl

Amalija Blurry (b. Emily Barry) is a Multimedia Artist whose work leverages physical and digital environments to explore complex concepts.

Her most recent project #ersatzGirl is photographic alchemy. In it, she layers hundreds of photographs over themselves to create hyper-realities - blurring the lines between photograph and painting and intentionally choosing Instagram as the gallery, in a commentary of social media's surreal tangential reality to our lives.

As a data visualization engineer, her work has been employed by the United Nations Ozone Secretariat to demonstrate worldwide reductions in ozone depleting chemicals.
